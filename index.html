ABOUT US: The Joseph and Jude sickle cell foundation was started by Evelyn Schiller in memory of her two little brothers, Joseph and Jude, who died of sickle cell disease at ages of 8 and 6 years respectively. The foundation was started to help people living with sickle cell disease in Eastern Uganda live healthy lives. The offices of the organisation are in Tororo district Hospital, Tororo District Uganda.
OUR MISSION: To provide high quality care for persons living with sickle cell in Eastern Uganda
OUR VISION: To see that people living with sickle cell live healthy and productive lives.
OUR OBJECTIVE: To mobilise resources to run a sicle cell clinic in Tororo district Hospital and to lobby government to increase resource allocation towards sickle cell care.
